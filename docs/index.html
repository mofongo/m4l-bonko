How it works

Detect

An onset detector tuned for guitar. Focus listens to the high-frequency bite of the pick attack, Sens sets how easily it fires, and Retrig keeps fast strumming from machine-gunning triggers. A meter and trigger LED show exactly what it hears.

Shape

Four independent slots. Each transient fires an attack/decay envelope with its own Min/Max range — set Min above Max to duck instead of swell. Prob makes slots fire by chance, and Rnd rolls new random envelope times on every hit.

Map

Click Map, then click any parameter on any device in your Live set — filter cutoff, reverb mix, grain size, anything. Powered by Live's native parameter mapping, so bindings survive saving and reloading your set.

Install

  1. Download Bonko.amxd One self-contained file — no externals, no packages, Apple Silicon native.
  2. Drop it on an audio track in Ableton Live Anywhere in the chain. Your audio passes through untouched.
  3. Map something and play Raise Sens until the LED follows your picking, then let your effects react.
